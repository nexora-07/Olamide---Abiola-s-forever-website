HOW WE MET


Abiola's POV

On a visit to Ife, I noticed her serving in church with such quiet confidence that I couldn’t help asking about her. Months later, conference preparations gave me the excuse to start conversations with her, and her calm composure drew me in.

After the conference, I messaged to appreciate her, and our chats soon became long calls and growing friendship. With time, I knew she was the one, but I stayed patient so she could be comfortable first.

In November 2022, I finally told her how I felt, though I asked her not to answer yet. I stayed consistent, and by December I sensed her heart had opened. So I asked again and she gave me the sweetest YES, and that YES brought us here today.


Olamide's POV

The first time I saw Biola was in Church. We had our first conversation while preparing for Osun Baptist Conference in Session Parade.

At first, it was R.A Director to Lydia conversations. After a while, we got very comfortable talking to each other. He asked me out on a date and said he wanted me to be his girlfriend, but told me not to reply immediately.

Our friendship continued and even got more personal. On 27th December 2022, he asked me again to be his girlfriend — and I said YES. The most convincing YES I ever said.
